About the site

Website building & maintenance is done via a home-made site generator written in C, by yours truly.

The color scheme of this website is: #260d1a and #d47d7d & the image in the header/footer is taken from Paul Klee's Forgetful Angel.

All posts are dated using the traditional Korean calendar.

All textual content is written either in GNU Emacs or Vim. After a long time (and an excellent experience) using pandoc and writing everything in Markdown, I started to want to write HTML directly again. Also, I wanted to have my site generator as independent as possible. This layer of added friction is appealing to me in an obscure way, and maybe tells you more about me than the list of interests above does :)
